---
title: EV Items and Usage
description: Overview of EVs and related items
---

EVs (Effort Values) are hidden stats that boost specific attributes like Attack or Speed, enhancing your Pokémon’s battle performance.
You earn EVs during duels based on your Pokémon and opponents.

From the shop, you can buy:
Vitamins to increase an EV stat by 10 points.
Berries to decrease an EV stat by 10 points.

Using EVs and these items helps customize and strengthen your Pokémon for battles.
