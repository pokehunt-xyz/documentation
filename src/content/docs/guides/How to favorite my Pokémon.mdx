---
title: How to Favorite My Pokémon
description: Marking a Pokémon as your favorite
---

To mark a Pokémon as your favorite, use the command:

/favorite 1
Here, 1 refers to the of the Pokémon ID.

You can also use:
-1 to favorite the latest Pokémon you caught
0 to favorite the currently selected Pokémon

Marking a Pokémon as favorite helps you keep track of your most valued or preferred Pokémon.
