---
title: How to Give a Nickname to My Pokémon
description: Assigning nicknames to your Pokémon
---

To give your Pokémon a nickname, use the command:
/nickname 1 BulbaGoat

Here, 1 is the Pokémon ID, and BulbaGoat is the nickname you want to assign.

After naming, you can use the nickname as the Pokémon’s ID in commands, for example:
/select BulbaGoat
/info BulbaGoat
