---
title: Nature Modifiers
description: Changing your Pokémon’s nature for stat bonuses
---

Nature Modifiers allow you to change your Pokémon’s nature for 1500 credits each.

Each nature increases one stat by 10% while decreasing another by 10%, helping you customize your Pokémon’s strengths and weaknesses.

You can purchase Nature Modifiers from the shop by selecting any pokemon by /select.
