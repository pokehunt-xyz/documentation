---
title: Mega Evolution
description: Unlocking and using Mega Evolution for Pokémon
---

Mega Evolution is a powerful transformation that boosts a Pokémon’s stats and changes its appearance during battles.
To Mega Evolve, a Pokémon must hold its specific Mega Stone , A special item tied to that Pokémon species.

To unlock Mega Evolution for any Pokémon, you need to purchase access from the shop.
Each Mega Evolution access costs 10,000 credits.

Note: For Primal Evolutions, select "Mega Evolution" from the shop as well.

Credits required for Mega Evolution access are deducted from your balance, so ensure you have enough before buying.
