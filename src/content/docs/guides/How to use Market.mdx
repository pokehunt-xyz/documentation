---
title: How to Use Market
description: Buying and selling Pokémon in the market
---

If you want to buy a Pokémon, use:

/market search or /market search pokeName , To find Pokémon using filters.

After finding one, run: /market view , To see its details. 

If you wish to buy it, use: /market buy 

To sell your Pokémon, use: /market create , to list it for sale with a set price.

To view all your Pokémon listed for sale, run: /market listings , and manage your sales easily.
