---
title: How to Duel
description: Starting and winning Pokémon battles
---

To start a duel, type:
/duel and tag your opponent, or reply to your opponent’s message with /duel to initiate a battle between your selected Pokémon.

Winning a duel rewards your Pokémon with XP based on the difficulty of the battle.
Before dueling, make sure to use /learn to equip specific moves to your Pokémon for better performance.
