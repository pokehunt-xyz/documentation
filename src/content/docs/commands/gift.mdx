---
title: Gift
description: Gift someone a Pokémon
---

Use the /gift command to send a Pokémon from your collection to another user.
You need to reply to the message of the user you want to gift the Pokémon to.

Usage:
/gift -1 , 
Gift your most recently caught Pokémon.

/gift BulbaGoat , 
Gift the Pokémon with the nickname "BulbaGoat".

/gift 0 ,
Gift your currently selected Pokémon.

/gift 3 ,
Gift the Pokémon in the 3rd slot of your collection.

This command allows you to share Pokémon with others simply by replying to their message and specifying which Pokémon to give.
