---
title: Release
description: Release (delete) a Pokémon
---

Use the /release command to permanently release (delete) a Pokémon from your collection.

Usage:
/release <id> , 
Releases the Pokémon with the specified ID.

/release -1 , 
Release your latest obtained Pokémon.

/release BulbaGoat , 
Release the Pokémon with the nickname "BulbaGoat".

/release 0 , 
Release your currently selected Pokémon.

/release 3 , 
Release your 3rd Pokémon in your collection
