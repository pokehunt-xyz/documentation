---
title: Auction listings
description: List the Pokémon you have on auction
---

To view the Pokémon you have currently listed on auction, use the command:

/auction listings , 
This command shows all your Pokémon that are up for auction.

To view a specific page or the latest page of your auction listings, use:

/auction listings -1 , 
This will display the most recent page of your active auction listings, helping you keep track of your ongoing auctions.
