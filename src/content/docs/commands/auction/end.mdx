---
title: Auction end
description: End the auction of your Pokémon
---

The /auction end \<auctionID\> command lets you manually end an ongoing auction for your Pokémon before the automatic timer runs out.
\<auctionID\>: The unique ID of the auction you want to end.

For example:
/auction end d9b , 
This will end the auction with ID d9b .
