---
title: Auction bid
description: Bid for a Pokémon on auction
---

To place a bid on a Pokémon auction, use the command:
/auction bid <auctionID> <amount> , 

<auctionID>: The unique ID of the auction you want to bid on.
<amount>: The amount of credits you want to bid.

For example:
/auction bid d9b 100 , 
This bids 100 credits on the auction with ID d9b.
