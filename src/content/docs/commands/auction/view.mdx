---
title: Auction view
description: View the details of a Pokémon on auction
---

To view detailed information about a Pokémon currently on auction, use the command:
/auction view <auctionID> , 
<auctionID> is the unique identifier of the auction you want to check.

For example:
/auction view d9b , 
This command will display details such as the Pokémon’s stats, current highest bid, and the time remaining on the auction. 
It helps you decide whether you want to place a bid or track an auction you are interested in.
