---
title: Invite
description: View the invite link and support server
---

Use the command:
/invite , 
to get the invite link for the PokéHunt bot as well as the link to its support server.

This command helps you:
Add PokéHunt to your own Discord or Telegram server.
Join the official support community where you can get help, updates, and interact with other players and the developers.
It’s the best way to share the bot and stay connected with the latest news and support.
