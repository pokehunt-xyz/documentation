---
title: Shop
description: Buy stones, bracelets, forms and rare candies for your Pokémon
---

Use the /shop command to view and buy various items for your Pokémon such as evolution stones, bracelets, forms, and rare candies.

Usage:
/shop , 
View the shop for your currently selected Pokémon.

/shop -1 , 
View the shop for your latest Pokémon.

/shop <nickname> , 
View the shop for a specific Pokémon by its nickname, e.g., /shop BulbaGoat.

/shop 0 ,
View the shop for your selected Pokémon (same as /shop).

/shop <number> , 
View the shop for the Pokémon in the specified position, e.g., /shop 3 for the third Pokémon.
