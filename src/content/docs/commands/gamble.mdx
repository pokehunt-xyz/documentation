---
title: Gamble
description: Gamble your credits with others
---

Use the /gamble \<amount\> command to gamble your credits with another user.

Important:
You must reply to the user’s message you want to gamble with in order to initiate the gamble.

Usage example:
Reply to a user’s message and type:
/gamble 250 , 
This will gamble 250 credits with that user.

Both users risk the specified amount of credits.
The outcome is decided randomly , you can either win or lose the credits wagered.
Gambling is a quick and exciting way to try and increase your credits but remember it involves risk.
Make sure to gamble responsibly and only what you can afford to lose!
