Use the command:
/guide ,
to view a detailed guide on how to use PokéHunt.

This guide covers:
How to start your Pokémon journey by picking a starter.
How to catch Pokémon when they randomly spawn while chatting.
How to hunt Pokémon with cooldowns and manage your collection.
How to use the auction, market, and trading systems to buy, sell, and exchange Pokémon.
How to complete quests and daily challenges for rewards.
How to level up Pokémon with rare candies and other items.

This command helps new and returning players understand the bot’s features and get the most out of PokéHunt.
